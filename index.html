Ch 1: Chemical Reactions & Equations

1. Chemical Reaction (रासायनिक अभिक्रिया):

Aisa process jisme naye properties wale naye substances bante hain.

2. Balancing Equation (समीकरण संतुलित करना):

Law of Conservation of Mass ke mutabik dono taraf atoms ki sankhya barabar honi chahiye.

Example: 3Fe + 4H₂O → Fe₃O₄ + 4H₂

3. Types of Reactions:

  • Combination: A + B → AB (Do milkar ek banna)
  • Decomposition: AB → A + B (Ek tootkar do banna)
  • Displacement: Fe + CuSO₄ → FeSO₄ + Cu (Zyada reactive metal kam reactive ko hata deta hai)
Ch 2: Acids, Bases and Salts

1. Acids vs Bases:

Acids: Swaad mein khatte, H⁺ ions dete hain, blue litmus ko red karte hain.

Bases: Swaad mein kadwe, OH⁻ ions dete hain, red litmus ko blue karte hain.

2. pH Scale:

0 se 14 tak hota hai. 7 se kam = Acid, 7 = Neutral (Pani), 7 se zyada = Base.

3. Important Formulas:

Bleaching Powder: CaOCl₂ Baking Soda: NaHCO₃ Washing Soda: Na₂CO₃·10H₂O Plaster of Paris (POP): CaSO₄·½H₂O
Ch 3: Metals and Non-metals

1. Physical Properties:

Metals sonorous, malleable aur ductile hote hain. Non-metals brittle hote hain.

2. Amphoteric Oxides (उभयधर्मी ऑक्साइड):

Jo acid aur base dono se react karke salt aur water banate hain.

Examples: Al₂O₃ aur ZnO

3. Ionic Compounds:

Electrons ke transfer se bante hain. Inka melting point bahut high hota hai.

Ch 4: Carbon and its Compounds

1. Covalent Bonding:

Carbon ka atomic number 6 hai (2,4). Ye electrons ki sharing karke covalent bond banata hai.

2. Versatile Nature of Carbon:

  • Catenation: Carbon-carbon lambi chain banane ki property.
  • Tetravalency: 4 valency hone ke karan 4 alag atoms se jud sakta hai.

3. Hydrocarbons:

Saturated: Single bond (Alkane: CₙH₂ₙ₊₂). Unsaturated: Double/Triple bond (Alkene/Alkyne).

Ch 5: Life Processes (जैव प्रक्रम)

1. Nutrition (पोषण):

Autotrophic: Plants jo Photosynthesis se apna khana khud banate hain. (6CO₂ + 6H₂O → C₆H₁₂O₆ + 6O₂)

Heterotrophic: Animals jo dusro par nirbhar hain.

2. Respiration (श्वसन):

Glucose ka breakdown karke ATP (energy) banana. Mitochondria ko cell ka powerhouse kehte hain.

3. Human Excretion:

Kidney ke andar "Nephrons" hote hain jo khoon ko saaf karke urine banate hain.

Ch 6: Control and Coordination

1. Nervous System:

Neuron body ka sabsb lamba cell hai jo signals brain tak pahunchata hai. Do neurons ke bich ke gap ko Synapse kehte hain.

2. Reflex Action:

Achanak hone wala response jo seedhe Spinal Cord control karta hai.

3. Plant Hormones:

  • Auxin & Gibberellin: Growth badhate hain.
  • Abscisic Acid: Growth ko rokta hai (pattiyan murjhana).
Ch 7: How do Organisms Reproduce?

1. Asexual Reproduction:

  • Binary Fission: Amoeba mein ek se do banna.
  • Budding: Hydra mein chota bud nikalna.
  • Regeneration: Planaria ka kata hua hissa fir se ugna.

2. Sexual Reproduction:

Male gamete (Sperm) aur Female gamete (Ova) milkar Zygote banate hain.

3. Contraception Methods:

Condoms (Barrier), Copper-T (IUCD), aur Surgical (Vasectomy/Tubectomy) family planning ke liye use hote hain.

Ch 8: Heredity (आनुवंशिकता)

1. Mendel's Experiment:

Gregor Mendel ko Father of Genetics kehte hain. Unhone Pea Plant (matar ke paudhe) par experiment kiya tha.

2. Monohybrid Cross:

F2 generation mein Phenotypic ratio hamesha 3:1 aata hai.

3. Sex Determination in Humans:

Humans mein 23 pairs chromosomes hote hain. 22 pairs same hote hain, 23rd pair sex chromosome hota hai (Male: XY, Female: XX). Bachhe ka gender father ke chromosome (X ya Y) par nirbhar karta hai.

Ch 9: Light - Reflection & Refraction

1. Reflection (परावर्तन):

Angle of Incidence hamesha Angle of Reflection ke barabar hota hai (∠i = ∠r).

2. Formulas:

Mirror Formula: 1/f = 1/v + 1/u Lens Formula: 1/f = 1/v - 1/u Magnification: m = -v/u (Mirror) | m = v/u (Lens) Power of Lens: P = 1/f (in meters) (Unit: Dioptre)

3. Refractive Index:

n = c/v (Speed of light in vacuum / Speed of light in medium).

Ch 10: Human Eye & Colorful World

1. Defects of Vision:

  • Myopia (Near-sightedness): Paas ka dikhta hai, door ka nahi. Correction: Concave Lens.
  • Hypermetropia (Far-sightedness): Door ka dikhta hai, paas ka nahi. Correction: Convex Lens.
  • Presbyopia: Budhapa mein dono kharab hona. Correction: Bifocal Lens.

2. Prism Dispersion:

White light ka 7 rangon (VIBGYOR) mein tootna dispersion kehlata hai.

3. Atmospheric Refraction:

Stars ka टिमटिमाना (twinkling) aur Advance sunrise isi ke karan hota hai.

Ch 11: Electricity (विद्युत)

1. Ohm's Law:

Constant temperature par Potential Difference current ke directly proportional hota hai.

V = IR (R = Resistance, Unit: Ohm Ω)

2. Resistance Combination:

Series (श्रेणीक्रम): R_s = R₁ + R₂ + R₃ Parallel (पार्वक्रम): 1/R_p = 1/R₁ + 1/R₂ + 1/R₃

3. Joule's Heating Effect:

H = I²Rt (Electrical energy ka heat mein badalna).

Ch 12: Magnetic Effects of Current

1. Magnetic Field Lines:

North pole se nikalti hain aur South pole par khatam hoti hain. Ye lines kabhi ek dusre ko intersect (kaat-ti) nahi hain.

2. Right Hand Thumb Rule:

Angutha current ki direction dikhata hai aur ungliyan magnetic field ki direction.

3. Solenoid (परिनालिका):

Insulated copper wire ki coil jisme se current pass karne par wo temporary magnet (electromagnet) ban jaati hai.

Ch 13: Our Environment

1. Ecosystem Components:

Biotic (Living: Plants, Animals) aur Abiotic (Non-living: Air, Water, Soil).

2. 10% Energy Law:

Ek trophic level se dusre level tak sirf 10% energy hi transfer hoti hai, baki 90% loss ho jaati hai.

3. Ozone Layer Depletion:

Ozone (O₃) UV rays ko rokti hai. Isko CFCs (Chlorofluorocarbons) gas nuksan pahunchati hai.
